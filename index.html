Dose

Gy / rad conversions (assume QF = 1 for photons/β)

When quality factor QF ≈ 1 (common for γ/β), 1 Gy ≈ 1 Sv and 1 rad ≈ 1 rem. For neutrons/α, this does not hold—don’t use these cross-conversions.

Dose rate

CPM ⇄ µSv/h (approx)

CPM→dose depends on your tube & energy. Use your own factor if you’ve calibrated.

Example factors (very rough): SBM-20 ≈ 175 CPM per µSv/h; pancake probes vary widely. Don’t use for safety decisions.
